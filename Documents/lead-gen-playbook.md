# Lead Generation Playbook

Based on Hormozi's $100M Leads framework: Core Four channels + Lead Magnet architecture.

---

## The Lead Gen Hierarchy

Always activate in this sequence. Later channels amplify earlier ones.

```
1. Warm Outreach (fastest to first dollar)
2. Content Machine (builds compounding authority)
3. Cold Outreach (scalable when offer is proven)
4. Paid Ads (amplifies what already converts)
```

---

## Channel 1: Warm Outreach

**Who:** People who already know you (past clients, colleagues, network, followers)

**Goal:** Book a conversation — not sell. You're checking if they have the problem, not pitching.

### Warm Outreach Script Template

```
Subject/Opening: [Personalized observation about them]

"Hey [Name] — noticed [specific thing about their situation]. 

Quick question: are you still dealing with [specific problem your offer solves]?

I've been working on something that's helped [2-3 people like them] 
[achieve specific result] in [timeframe]. 

Would it make sense to grab 15 minutes this week to see if it might 
apply to your situation?"
```

**Rules:**
- Never pitch in the first message
- Always end with a yes/no question
- Follow up 3 times over 7 days if no response
- Target: 10+ genuine warm conversations per day

---

## Channel 2: Content Machine

**Core principle:** Give away your process, not just your results. Document, don't just create.

### Content Pillars (pick 2-3 for your niche)

| Pillar | Example Content |
|---|---|
| Education | "How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]" |
| Mistakes | "The #1 error [ICP] make when [doing X]" |
| Behind-the-scenes | "How I [did something hard] — real numbers" |
| Social proof | "Client went from X to Y — here's exactly how" |
| Controversy | "Why [popular belief in your industry] is wrong" |

### Platform Strategy by Stage

**Stage 1 (0 → 1,000 followers):** Pick ONE platform. Post daily. Engage obsessively.

**Stage 2 (1,000 → 10,000):** Repurpose to 2 platforms. Start building email list.

**Stage 3 (10,000+):** Full Core Four, paid amplification of top organic content.

### Content → Lead Conversion

Every piece of content needs ONE call-to-action that routes to a lead magnet:
- "DM me [keyword] for the free [template/checklist]"
- "Comment [word] and I'll send you the [tool]"
- "Link in bio → free [lead magnet]"

## Lead Magnet Design

### The 5 Non-Negotiables

1. **Solves one specific problem** (not a general overview)
2. **Delivers value in < 10 minutes** (not a 200-page ebook)
3. **Makes the next step obvious** (leads naturally to your paid offer)
4. **Has a clear, benefit-driven name** (not "My Free Guide")
5. **Is format-matched to your audience** (busy founders → checklist, not video course)

### Lead Magnet Name Formula

```
"The [Adjective] [Format] for [ICP] to [Outcome] in [Timeframe] Without [Pain]"
```

Examples:
- "The 5-Minute Offer Audit Checklist for SaaS Founders to Double Conversions Without Touching Their Ads"
- "The 3-Step Lead Magnet Template for Coaches to Get 50 Qualified Leads Per Month Without Cold DMs"

### Lead Magnet by Funnel Stage

| Awareness Stage | Magnet Type | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Problem-unaware | Short quiz | "Is your offer the reason your ads aren't working?" |
| Problem-aware | Checklist / tool | "The Grand Slam Offer Scorecard" |
| Solution-aware | Case study | "How [niche] went from $0 to $23k/mo in 61 days" |
| Product-aware | Demo / trial | "Try the AI offer optimizer free for 7 days" |

## Channel 3: Cold Outreach

**When to activate:** After you have 3+ documented case studies and a proven offer.

### Cold Outreach Formula

```
[Hyper-specific observation about THEM] + [Bridge to outcome you deliver] + [Social proof] + [Low-friction ask]
```

**Email example:**
```
Subject: [Their company] + [specific trigger I noticed]

Hi [Name],

Noticed [specific thing about their business — job posting, content, 
product launch, funding announcement].

We specialize in helping [their type of business] [achieve specific outcome] 
in [timeframe]. Recently helped [similar company] [specific result with number].

Worth a 15-minute call to see if there's a fit?

[Your name]
```

**Rules:**
- Personalize the first line — never send a template that reads like a template
- One ask per email
- Follow-up sequence: Day 1 → Day 3 → Day 7 → Day 14 (4 touches max)
- Target: 20–50 cold contacts per day at scale

---

## Channel 4: Paid Ads

**When to activate:** Only after organic channels convert. Ads amplify — they don't fix.

### The 3-Step Paid Playbook

**Step 1 — Lead to lead magnet** (not directly to your paid offer)
- Lower CPC, builds trust, nurtures before selling
- Best lead magnet for ads: quiz or short-form tool

**Step 2 — Retarget with social proof**
- Hit lead magnet visitors with 3 case study videos
- Build the "I've seen them everywhere" effect

**Step 3 — Convert with offer**
- Only after 3+ touchpoints
- Use urgency (cohort close date, price increase)

### Budget Allocation (for early stage)

| Channel | % of Paid Budget |
|---|---|
| Lead magnet acquisition | 60% |
| Retargeting (social proof) | 25% |
| Conversion (offer) | 15% |

## Lead Magnet Templates

### Template 1: Checklist

```
Title: "The [N]-Point [Niche] Audit: [Outcome]"

Format:
[ ] Item 1 — [What to check] + [Why it matters]
[ ] Item 2 — [What to check] + [Why it matters]
...
[ ] Item N

Bottom CTA: "Score 8+? You're ready to [next step]. 
Score below 8? Book a free [next step call] at [link]."
```

### Template 2: Calculator / Scorecard

```
Input variables: [3-5 inputs the user already knows]
Output: Their personalized score + what it means
Segmented results: "If 0-30... If 31-60... If 61-100..."
CTA tied to score range
```

### Template 3: Mini-Case Study

```
Structure:
1. Before state (2 sentences — vivid, specific pain)
2. The turning point (what changed)
3. The method (your framework in 3-4 steps)
4. The result (specific number + timeframe)
5. CTA: "Want this for [their niche]? [Next step]"
```
