|---|---|

| First 500 users acquisition campaign | $3,000 |
| 90-day bug fix support | $4,500 |
| Analytics dashboard setup | $1,500 |
| **Total Value** | **$29,500** |

**Price:** $4,997

**Risk Reversal:** "If we don't deliver a published app in 30 days, you pay nothing. We also cover App Store fees."

**Offer Score:** ~480,000

## Common Patterns in High-Scoring Grand Slam Offers

1. **Specificity wins** — "lose 20 lbs" vs. "fit into your size 6 dress before your May 15 wedding"
2. **Stack > single product** — Bundle 5 things, show individual values, price below combined value
3. **Guarantee removes the #1 objection** — "What if it doesn't work?" must be answered before pricing
4. **Done-for-you is a multiplier** — Every DFY element drops Effort Score dramatically
5. **Real scarcity converts skeptics** — Manufactured urgency gets ignored; genuine capacity constraints work
