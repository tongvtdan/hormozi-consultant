## Example 1 — AI-First Mobile App Studio (Solo Founder / B2C)

**Market:** Small business owners who need a custom mobile app but can't afford $50k+ dev shops
*(Pain: high ✅ | Purchasing power: medium-high ✅ | Easy to target: yes ✅ | Growing: yes ✅)*

**Niche:** 4-level niche: Mobile Apps → iOS → Small Business → E-commerce Stores Under $2M Revenue

**Dream Outcome:** "A fully functional iOS app live in your App Store account in 30 days, generating your first app revenue — without you writing a line of code or managing a developer"

**Problem → Solution Matrix:**
| Obstacle | Solution/Deliverable |
|---|---|
| Don't know what to build | Discovery workshop (2 hours, DWY) |
| Can't afford a developer | AI-assisted build process |
| Don't know how to submit to App Store | We handle submission and compliance |
| No one will download it | 500-user acquisition campaign (Week 4) |
| Breaks after launch | 90-day bug fix support (DFY) |
| Can't read the data | Analytics dashboard setup + training |

**Delivery Cube:**
- Group: 1-on-1 | Effort: Done-for-you | Support: Zoom + chat + phone
- Consumption: Live workshops + async | Speed: 30-day sprint model

**Trim & Stack (Value Table):**
| Deliverable | Standalone Value |
|---|---|
| Custom iOS app (design + build + deploy) | $18,000 |
| App Store optimization (ASO) | $2,500 |
| First 500 users acquisition campaign | $3,000 |
| 90-day bug fix support | $4,500 |
| Analytics dashboard setup | $1,500 |
| Discovery workshop | $1,000 |
| **Total Stated Value** | **$30,500** |

**Price:** $4,997 (6:1 value gap)

**Bonus Stack:**
- Bonus #1: "The App Store Optimization Blueprint" — value $997
- Bonus #2: "First 30 Users Playbook" — value $497

**Risk Reversal:** "If we don't deliver a published, working app in 30 days, you pay nothing. We also cover Apple Developer fees."

**Scarcity:** 3 client spots per month (genuine capacity constraint)

**MAGIC Name:** "30-Day App Launch Program for E-commerce Owners"

**Offer Score:** ~480,000 ⭐⭐⭐ Elite

## Example 4 — Agency Grand Slam Offer (from Book — Real Example)

**Source:** Directly from $100M Offers Chapter 3

**Old Commoditized Offer:** $1,000 down, then $1,000/month retainer for lead generation services

**Grand Slam Offer Version:**
"Pay one time. No recurring fee. No retainer. Just cover ad spend. I'll generate leads and work your leads for you. **Only pay me if people show up.** I'll guarantee 20 people in your first month, or your next month is free. I'll also provide all the best practices from businesses exactly like yours — daily sales coaching, tested scripts, proven price points, sales recordings — and everything you need to sell and fulfill your customers. I give you the entire playbook for your industry, absolutely free, just for becoming a client."

**Risk Reversal:** 20-client guarantee or next month free (conditional)

**Result from book:** 2.5x more ad responses + 2.5x higher close rate + 4x higher front-end price = **22.4x more cash collected upfront vs. commodity version**

**Offer Score:** ~690,000 ⭐⭐⭐ Elite

**Key lesson:** Same service. Same delivery. Same team. Different offer = 22.4x more revenue.

## How to Apply These Examples

1. **Match your niche** to the closest example type
2. **Copy the structure**, not the content — especially the Value Stack format
3. **Use the Problem → Solution Matrix** as the building block (list every obstacle)
4. **Price the Delivery Cube modality** — DFY > DWY > DIY in perceived value
5. **Risk reversal type** should match your confidence in results
6. **Always use MAGIC naming** — the same offer with a MAGIC name converts dramatically better
