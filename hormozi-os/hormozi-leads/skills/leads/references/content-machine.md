## The 5 Core Platforms — Full Protocol

### Platform 1 — Short-Form Video (TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts)

**Format:** 30–90 seconds
**Frequency:** Daily (5–7x/week)
**Goal:** Organic reach → cold audience → awareness

**Structure:**
```
[0–3 sec]  HOOK — pattern interrupt or bold claim
[3–20 sec] TENSION — amplify the problem or tease the resolution
[20–60 sec] LESSON — one insight or framework (not five)
[60–90 sec] CTA — "follow for more" or "link in bio for [lead magnet]"
```

**Production rules:**
- No intro (start mid-sentence or mid-action)
- First 2 words must earn the watch
- Caption must be able to stand alone as text content
- Always include a text overlay on the hook

**Repurposing chain:**
Short video → clip to X thread → transcribe to LinkedIn post → clip to newsletter

---

### Platform 2 — Long-Form Video (YouTube)

**Format:** 10–20 minutes
**Frequency:** 1x/week
**Goal:** SEO authority + long-form trust + case study proof

**Structure:**
```
[0–30 sec]  Hook — what they will get from watching
[30 sec–2 min] Problem statement — why this matters NOW
[2–12 min]  Core content — framework / case study / step-by-step
[12–15 min] Proof — result, data, testimonial
[15–20 min] CTA — subscribe + lead magnet
```

**SEO title formula:**
```
[Number] + [What] + [For whom] + [Without/Despite pain]
```
Examples:
- "3 Lead Magnets That Generated $500K for Service Businesses Without Ads"
- "How to Book 10 Sales Calls Per Week With No Audience"

**Thumbnail formula:** Big contrasting text + expressive face + single visual element

---

### Platform 3 — LinkedIn / Newsletter

**Format:** 300–800 words (LinkedIn), 500–1,500 words (newsletter)
**Frequency:** 3×/week (LinkedIn), 1×/week (newsletter)
**Goal:** B2B trust + professional credibility + nurture

**LinkedIn Post Structure:**
```
[Line 1]    HOOK — bold statement or counterintuitive claim (no "Hey everyone!")
[Line 2]    BLANK LINE (forces "see more" click)
[Lines 3–8] BODY — framework, story, or case study
[Line 9]    INSIGHT — the key takeaway
[Line 10]   CTA — "What's your biggest challenge with [X]? Comment below."
```

**Newsletter Structure:**
```
Subject line: [Specific promise or curiosity gap]
Preview text: [Completes or extends the subject line]

Para 1: The problem (make them nod)
Para 2: Why current approaches fail
Para 3: Your framework / insight
Para 4: Proof / example
Para 5: CTA to lead magnet or offer
Signature: [Your name] + [Lead magnet link]
```

---

### Platform 4 — X (Twitter)

**Format:** Thread (5–15 tweets) or single sharp insight
**Frequency:** Daily
**Goal:** Distribution, reach, thought leadership positioning

**Thread structure:**
```
Tweet 1:  Bold claim / result / hook (this is your headline)
Tweet 2:  Context — why this matters
Tweets 3–N: One point per tweet — crisp, numbered
Final tweet: Summary + CTA (follow / link to magnet)
```

**Single tweet formula:**
```
[Counterintuitive statement].

Here's why: [one-sentence explanation].
```

### Platform 5 — Community (Skool / Discord / Circle)

**Format:** Daily discussions, weekly AMAs, resource drops
**Frequency:** Daily engagement
**Goal:** Community trust, high-conversion leads, network-effect proof

**Community content types:**
- Resource drops (free templates, checklists, recordings)
- Question threads ("What's your #1 lead gen challenge this week?")
- Case study posts (your results OR member results)
- AMAs (answer everything — turns lurkers into believers)
- Challenge activations (7-day sprint, accountability thread)

## The Hook Library — 7 Templates

### Template 1 — Contrast
```
"Most [ICP] [common belief / action]. The top 1% [opposite truth]."
```
Example: "Most agencies chase new clients. The top 1% spend 80% of their time increasing client LTV."

**Emotional trigger:** Status (aspiration to be in the top 1%)

---

### Template 2 — Number
```
"[Specific number] [outcome] in [specific timeframe] — here's exactly how."
```
Example: "47 qualified calls in 30 days — here's the exact 3-step system."

**Emotional trigger:** Greed + Curiosity

---

### Template 3 — Mistake
```
"The #1 mistake [ICP] make when [common action] (and how to fix it in [time])."
```
Example: "The #1 mistake coaches make when writing cold emails (and how to fix it in 10 minutes)."

**Emotional trigger:** Fear of missing out / getting it wrong

---

### Template 4 — Secret / Authority
```
"What [authority figure / most people] never tells you about [topic]."
```
Example: "What business schools never teach you about scaling a service business."

**Emotional trigger:** Curiosity + Insider status

---

### Template 5 — Story
```
"I [painful past state]. Today [transformed state]. Here's what changed."
```
Example: "I spent $40,000 on ads with 0 clients. Today I have a waitlist with $0 in ad spend. Here's what changed."

**Emotional trigger:** Belonging + Hope

---

### Template 6 — Question / Visualization
```
"What would you do if [scenario that forces visualization of the dream outcome]?"
```
Example: "What would you do if you woke up Monday with 15 new qualified leads waiting in your inbox?"

**Emotional trigger:** Greed + Dream activation

---

### Template 7 — Bold Claim
```
"[Counterintuitive statement that is provably true and creates cognitive dissonance]."
```
Example: "You don't need more traffic. You need a better lead magnet."

**Emotional trigger:** Curiosity + Tension

## The Headline Formula

### Direct Response Formula
```
[Action verb] + [Dream outcome] + [Timeframe] + [Without primary objection]
```
Example: "Generate 20 Qualified Leads Per Month Without Cold Calling or Paid Ads"

### MAGIC Formula (for lead magnet naming)
```
[Magnetic reason to act now] + [Avatar (specific ICP)] + [Goal/outcome] + [Time interval] + [Container word]
```
Example: "The 15-Minute Lead Machine Audit for B2B Service Owners Who Want 10 Qualified Calls This Week"

| Capacity | Minimum Viable | Full Machine |
|---|---|---|
| 1 person, side project | 3 posts/week (one platform) | 1 short video/day |
| Solopreneur, full-time | 1 short video/day + 3 LinkedIn posts/week | + 1 YouTube/week + newsletter |
| Team (2+ people) | Full machine on 2 platforms | All 5 platforms, repurpose cross-platform |

**Rule: Master one platform before adding a second.** Spreading too thin = zero platform momentum.

## Content → Lead Conversion Optimization

Every piece of content must have exactly **one** clear CTA that converts attention into a contact.

**CTA hierarchy by content type:**

| Content Type | Best CTA |
|---|---|
| Short video | "Link in bio for [lead magnet name]" |
| YouTube video | "Download the free [magnet] in the description" |
| LinkedIn post | "Comment [keyword] and I'll DM you the [magnet]" |
| Newsletter | "Click here to get [magnet]" |
| Thread/X post | "Reply 'yes' and I'll DM you" |
| Community post | "Grab the template in the resources tab" |

**"Comment [keyword]" tactic:** Triggers algorithm boost AND creates a permission-based DM list. High compliance, high reach.

## The Repurposing Matrix

One long-form piece of content → 10+ distribution assets:

| Source | Derivatives |
|---|---|
| 1 YouTube video (20 min) | 3 short clips, 1 LinkedIn post, 1 thread, 3 email sequences |
| 1 long-form case study | 5 short hooks, 1 LinkedIn carousel, 1 newsletter |
| 1 live Q&A / AMA | 5–10 short clips, 1 FAQ blog post, 1 newsletter |
| 1 client result | 1 case study post, 1 short testimonial clip, 1 email proof story |

**Rule:** Never let content live in only one place. Every piece should touch at least 3 channels.

## Metrics That Matter

| Metric | Benchmark | What It Tells You |
|---|---|---|
| Hook retention (3-second) | ≥ 70% | Is the hook working? |
| Average watch time | ≥ 40% | Is the content delivering? |
| Lead magnet CTR from content | ≥ 2–5% | Is the CTA compelling? |
| Email opt-in rate (from content CTA) | ≥ 30–50% | Is the landing page working? |
| Engaged lead → booked call | ≥ 10% | Is the nurture working? |

**Kill rule:** If a content format has < 1% CTR after 20 posts, kill the format and test a new hook structure.
