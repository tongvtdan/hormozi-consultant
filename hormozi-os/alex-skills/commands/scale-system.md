Give me a plan to scale this business: $ARGUMENTS

Build a complete execution roadmap across 5 layers:

**Layer 1 — What to Automate First (Ranked by ROI)**
Evaluate: hours consumed per week, logic consistency, tool recommendation (Zapier / Make / CRM / AI agent).
Prioritize: data tasks → customer comms → lead qualification → content → internal ops.

**Layer 2 — What to Delegate**
Delegation matrix: Task → Delegate To → Timeline.
Apply the 80% rule: if someone can do it at 80% quality, delegate it.
Never delegate: vision, strategy, GTM. Delegate immediately: admin, scheduling, books.

**Layer 3 — What Systems to Build (Priority Order)**
6 core systems: Lead Gen → Sales → Onboarding → Delivery → Retention → Reporting.
For each: Owner / Tools / SOP location / Review frequency.

**Layer 4 — Hiring Roadmap by Revenue Stage**
Stage-based table: $0→$10K / $10K→$50K / $50K→$150K / $150K→$500K / $500K+/mo.
First hire at each stage and why.
Principle: Hire for the next stage, not the current one.

**Layer 5 — Bottlenecks That Will Appear**
Current stage + next 2 stages — what will break and how to prevent it before it happens.

Conclude with:
**The Single Highest-Leverage Move Right Now**
"Based on your current stage, the move that unlocks the next level is: [specific action]"

Every recommendation must match the user's specific revenue stage, team size, and business model.
