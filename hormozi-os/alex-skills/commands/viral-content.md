Build a repeatable framework across 4 modules:

**Module 1 — 20 High-Converting Hooks**
Cover all 7 hook categories: Contrarian / Outcome / Pain / Curiosity / List / Story / Warning.
Every hook must name a specific audience, outcome, or pain. Weak: "5 things entrepreneurs wish they knew." Strong: "5 things $1M/yr consultants do that freelancers think are crazy."
Make all 20 copy-paste ready.

**Module 2 — 10 Content Formats**
Table: Format / Platform fit / Why it works / Output type.
Include: Myth-buster, Before/After, Framework reveal, Contrarian take, "I tried X" story, Step-by-step system, Case study, Hot take list, Failure breakdown, Trend reaction.

**Module 3 — Emotional Trigger Map**
Identify the top 3 triggers for this specific niche from: Fear / Status / Curiosity / Anger / Hope / Belonging / Surprise.
For each: what it activates + content mechanic to trigger it.

**Module 4 — Shareability Mechanics**
Answer: Why will people share this?
- Identity share: "Sharing this makes me look..."
- Tool share: "I'd share this because it's useful for..."
- Social currency: "Sharing this signals I'm..."

Include a repeatable weekly content cadence using the formats and hooks above.
