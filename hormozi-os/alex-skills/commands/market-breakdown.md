Run a full Alex Hormozi-style market breakdown for: $ARGUMENTS

Do NOT give surface insights.

Structure the output as follows:

**1. Market Sizing (TAM / SAM / SOM)**
Break into three levels with estimated dollar figures and rationale.

**2. Top 5 Trends Shaping Demand**
Identify macro and micro forces. Flag which are accelerating vs. plateauing. Note first-mover opportunities.

**3. Top 5 Underserved Opportunities**
Gaps between what customers want and what exists. Segments ignored by incumbents. Problems growing but unsolved at scale.

**4. Where Money Is Already Flowing**
Which players attract investment and spend. Which business models are winning. Which segments are saturated vs. undercapitalized.

**5. Strategic Entry Signal**
Single highest-leverage entry point framed as:
"The market is underserving [specific customer] who wants [specific outcome] but can't get it because [specific gap]."

Output as structured tables and labeled sections — never unstructured paragraphs.
