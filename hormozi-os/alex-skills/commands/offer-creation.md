Create a high-converting offer for: $ARGUMENTS

Build all 5 components of the Hormozi Offer Stack:

**1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)**
Who they are, what they desperately want, what they've already tried, what they fear, what makes them qualified (urgency + budget + authority).

**2. Value Proposition**
One sentence: "We help [ICP] go from [painful before] to [desired after] in [timeframe] without [biggest objection]."
Then expand with: primary outcome, secondary outcomes, speed, simplicity.

**3. Pricing Architecture — 3 Tiers**
| Tier    | Price | What's Included | Best For |
Price to value. Anchor premium first. Entry tier = momentum, not profit.

**4. Guarantee + Risk Reversal**
Choose: Outcome guarantee / Effort guarantee / Conditional guarantee.
Write the guarantee headline and supporting copy.

**5. Competitive Differentiation**
vs. DIY / vs. Top Competitor / vs. Doing Nothing.
State the Unfair Advantage — what competitors cannot copy quickly.

Format the output exactly like a landing page structure:
HERO → WHO THIS IS FOR → WHAT YOU GET → PRICING → GUARANTEE → WHY US.

Every element must be specific. "We help businesses grow" fails. "We help B2B SaaS founders under $5M ARR add $50K MRR in 90 days without hiring a sales team" passes.
