List the top 10 problems in: $ARGUMENTS

For each problem, score across all 4 axes:

**Axis 1 — Urgency (1–10)**
10 = needs solving today or they bleed money / lose customers / face legal risk.

**Axis 2 — Willingness to Pay (1–10)**
10 = customers already spending heavily on imperfect alternatives.

**Axis 3 — Growth Trajectory**
🔺 Accelerating / ➡️ Stable / 🔻 Declining

**Axis 4 — Complaint Volume**
🔴 High (active forums, social media, word-of-mouth) / 🟡 Medium / 🟢 Low

Output as a ranked table with all 4 axes scored. Then provide:
- **Priority Recommendation**: Which single problem to build a business around and why
- **Honorable Mentions**: Problems with high WTP but low urgency (upsell candidates) or high urgency but low WTP (avoid)

Every problem must be specific enough to name the exact customer, cost, and reason it's unsolved.
