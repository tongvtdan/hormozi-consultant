# Value Equation — Deep Reference
**Source: $100M Offers, Chapter 6 — Alex Hormozi**

```
Value = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
Perfect Score = 1,000,000
```

> Key math: Time and Effort are in the DENOMINATOR. Halving either DOUBLES the score.
> This makes denominator improvements 2–10x more impactful than numerator improvements of the same magnitude.

## Component Maximization Guide

### Dream Outcome — Maximize
- Use: "From [X] to [Y] by [specific date]"
- Add downstream benefits: "which means [next benefit] which means [identity shift]"
- Specificity matters: "lose 23 lbs" > "lose weight"
- Connect to status, freedom, relationships, identity

### Perceived Likelihood — Maximize
- Case studies with identical ICP (named > anonymous)
- Strong guarantees (unconditional > conditional > implied)
- Credentials, track record, third-party verification
- Social proof hierarchy: in-person > recorded > screenshots
- Specific success rates: "93% of clients" > "most clients"

### Time Delay — Minimize
- Add a Week 1 quick win (proof of concept, not full result)
- Create an onboarding sprint or fast-start kit
- Map the progress arc: Day 7 → Month 1 → Month 3
- DFY collapses perceived wait time dramatically

### Effort & Sacrifice — Minimize
- DFY is the single most powerful lever
- Replace instructions with templates
- Replace thinking with checklists
- Remove steps; simplify process
- Tools > trainings (consumption effort comparison)
