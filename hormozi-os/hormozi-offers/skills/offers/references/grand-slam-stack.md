## Worked Example 1 — B2B Lead Gen Agency (from Book)

**Old commodity offer:** $1,000 down + $1,000/month retainer

**Grand Slam version:**
> "Pay one time. No retainer. Just cover ad spend. I'll generate leads AND work your leads for you. Only pay me if people show up. I guarantee 20 in your first month or your next month is free. I'll also give you all the best practices from businesses exactly like yours — daily coaching, tested scripts, proven price points, recordings — and everything you need to sell and fulfill. You get the entire playbook for your industry, free, just for becoming a client."

**Delivery Cube:** DFY lead gen + DWY sales coaching + Recorded playbook library
**Guarantee:** 20-client conditional guarantee or next month free
**Result:** 2.5x ad responses × 2.5x close rate × 4x front-end price = **22.4x more cash upfront**

## Guarantee Type Selection Matrix

| Your Confidence | Offer Type | Recommended Guarantee |
|---|---|---|
| Very high | Productized service | Unconditional money-back |
| High | Coaching/consulting | Conditional (must complete program) |
| High | Agency | Performance/results-based |
| High | B2B | Delayed second payment |
| Medium | New offer | Service guarantee (keep working free) |
| Any | Premium/exclusive | Anti-guarantee ("all sales final") |

## Niche Pricing Reality

Same content, 4 specificity levels = 100x price difference:

| Level | Example | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Generic | "Time Management" | $19 |
| Category | "Time Management for Sales Pros" | $99 |
| Sub-category | "Time Management for B2B Outbound Reps" | $499 |
| Ultra-niche | "Time Management for B2B Power Tools Sales Reps" | $2,000 |

**Rule:** Under $10M/year — always niche down. The market can't be "too small" until you've extracted $10M from it.
